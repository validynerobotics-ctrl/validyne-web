YOUR APPLICATION

We don't sell off-the-shelf products. We design and build robotic systems around your specific operational requirements — from concept to commissioned system.

01

Autonomous Mobile Robots

Custom AMRs designed for your environment — indoor, outdoor, sloped terrain, shared traffic, heavy payload. We engineer navigation, sensing, and braking around your specific site conditions, not flat warehouse assumptions.

02

Arm-Based Applications

Multi-DOF robotic manipulators and arm systems for pick-and-place, assembly, inspection, and intervention tasks. Built around your payload, reach, and precision requirements.

03

Custom Robotic Systems

Ground-up design of robotic platforms for applications that don't fit standard categories. If your environment, payload, or task is unusual — that's exactly where we work best.

04

Motion & Actuation Systems

High-performance actuation, motor drive electronics, and motion control architectures. Designed for precision, reliability, and long field life in demanding environments.

05

Embedded Control & Software

Real-time firmware, control systems, and perception software for robotic platforms. From bare-metal motor control to full navigation stacks — written and owned by us.

06

R&D Engineering Partnership

Long-term technical partnerships for organisations that need deep robotics expertise. We embed with your team and take full ownership of the engineering problem.
